## Detailed Breakdown

### 1. "date night ideas at home"
**Title:** 50 At-Home Date Night Ideas (Fun & Easy)  
**Why:** Multi-source signal, massive search volume. "At home" is the #1 modifier — people want low-effort, no-babysitter-needed options.  
**Strategy:** Listicle format. Group by category (cooking, movies, games, creative, spa, outdoor backyard). Include supplies/links. Feature a printable checklist.  
**SERP:** Featured snippet likely (listicle), PAA boxes, image pack. Use numbered list format for snippet capture.

### 2. "date night ideas for married couples"
**Title:** Date Night Ideas for Married Couples (2026)  
**Why:** High intent from the married segment — they search specifically because generic lists feel aimed at new daters.  
**Strategy:** Tailor tone to long-term relationships. Include both budget-friendly and splurge options. Sections: reconnecting, adventure, learning together, at-home, seasonal.  
**SERP:** PAA dominant. Video carousel likely from YouTube competition.

### 3. "date night ideas for couples"
**Title:** 75 Best Date Night Ideas for Every Couple  
**Why:** Broader than "married" — captures dating couples too. Strong volume.  
**Strategy:** Mega-list with filters (budget, indoor/outdoor, adventurous/chill). Can cannibalize with #2 — consider one comprehensive page targeting both.  
**SERP:** Featured snippet, PAA, video carousel.

### 4. "date night ideas at home for married couples"
**Title:** At-Home Date Nights for Married Couples  
**Why:** Long-tail combo of #1 and #2 — very specific, lower competition, high conversion intent.  
**Strategy:** Sub-section of the at-home article OR standalone. Focus on reconnection themes, no-kids-needed activities, 30-min quick dates.  
**SERP:** Lower competition, easier to rank.

### 5. "date night ideas los angeles"
**Title:** Best Date Nights in LA (Local Guide 2026)  
**Why:** Strongest geo signal. LA has massive population + tourist demand.  
**Strategy:** Neighborhood-by-neighborhood guide. Include specific venue names, price ranges, reservation tips. Update seasonally.  
**SERP:** Local pack, map results, Google Business profiles dominate. Need strong local content.

### 6. "date night ideas chicago"
**Title:** Chicago Date Night Ideas: 40+ Spots  
**Why:** Second strongest geo signal after LA.  
**Strategy:** Similar to LA — neighborhood guide (Loop, Wicker Park, Lincoln Park, etc.). Seasonal Chicago content (rooftops in summer, cozy spots in winter).  
**SERP:** Local pack dominant.

### 7. "date night ideas las vegas"
**Title:** Las Vegas Date Night Beyond the Strip  
**Why:** Unique angle — most content is tourist-focused. Differentiate with local/off-strip options.  
**Strategy:** Mix of classic Strip experiences + hidden gems. Budget tiers. "Free" and "luxury" sections.  
**SERP:** Heavy ad competition from venues. Organic opportunity in long-form guides.

### 8. "date night ideas besides dinner"
**Title:** 30 Date Night Ideas That Aren't Dinner  
**Why:** Under-served query — people are bored of "go to a restaurant." High engagement potential.  
**Strategy:** Activity-focused: classes, escape rooms, outdoor adventures, creative arts, games, volunteering. Frame as alternatives.  
**SERP:** PAA-heavy. Featured snippet opportunity — few pages target this exact angle.

### 9. "date night ideas cheap"
**Title:** 25 Cheap Date Night Ideas Under $20  
**Why:** Budget-conscious segment is large and growing. High RPM from affiliate potential (products, kits, subscription boxes).  
**Strategy:** Specific price points per idea. Link to budget-friendly products/kits. Include truly free options too.  
**SERP:** Listicle snippets, PAA. Affiliate sites compete here.

### 10. "date night ideas unique"
**Title:** Unique Date Night Ideas You Haven't Tried  
**Why:** Differentiator — searchers have seen the standard lists and want something fresh.  
**Strategy:** Lean into unexpected ideas: stargazing apps, foraging walks, silent disco at home, theme nights, volunteer dates, local factory tours.  
**SERP:** Lower competition than generic. High share/save potential on social.
